Stoner rock from Malmö, Sweden.
Ready to kiss your ears.

Who?

Formed in 2012, Huanastone has been delivering songs with heartfelt lyrics, hard-hitting soulful rhythms and ominous heavy riffs reminding you of grunge from The 90's and the heavy rock coming out in The 70's.

Performing at various surf parties and mini-tours in and around Malmö, Sweden. With their new self-titled EP, recorded live in an abandoned school building from The 19th century in The deep woods of Sweden, they hand you music that move your body as well as your mind.
